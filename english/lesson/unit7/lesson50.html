1. Steve: Hi, there, Emma. Do you want to go to the cinema with me? 🎬😊
Emma: I’m sorry, Steve, but I can’t.🙁
Steve: Why? What’s the matter? Are you ill?❓🤒
Emma: Yeah. It’s probably the burgers and the chips I ate at the fast food restaurant.🍔🍟😣
Steve: You shouldn’t eat junk food. It’s bad for you.🚫🍕
Emma: I know.😔
Steve: Get well soon. Bye.🌷👋


2. Steve: Hey, Debbie, what’s up?👋🙂
Debbie: Nothing much.😐
Steve: Don’t tell me you’re ill, too!😬🤒
Debbie: Yes! My head hurts and I have got a fever.🤕🌡️
Steve: You should go to bed, then.🛏️💤
Debbie: Thank you.🙏
Steve: OK. Take care. Bye.💛👋


3. Steve: Hi, Liz. Do you want to go to the cinema?👋🎬
Liz: Oh, I can’t, Steve. I went swimming yesterday and I got water in my ear and I can’t hear now.🏊‍♀️👂💧😣
Steve: You should be careful when you go swimming. All right, then, get well soon. Bye.🌊⚠️🤗
Liz: Bye, Steve.👋😊

4. Steve: Hello, Sue. Say yes.👋😄
Sue: What?🤨
Steve: Just say yes. Say you’re coming with me to the cinema.🎬🙏
Sue: I can’t Steve. I don’t feel well.😞🤒
Steve: Why? What’s the matter? Have you got toothache?❓🦷😕
Sue: No. My throat hurts really badly.😣🧣
Steve: All of you are ill today.😅🤧
Sue: I’m sorry, Steve.😔
Steve: OK. Bye.👋

1. Стив: Привет, Эмма. Хочешь пойти со мной в кино? 🎬😊
