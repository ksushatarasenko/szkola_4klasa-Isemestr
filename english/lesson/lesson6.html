The New Student

story time

Frame 1: Yu Lin’s dad is dropping her off at her new school and is saying goodbye. She is scared because she hasn’t got any friends at the school. Her dad tries to make her feel better.

Frame2: Two girls approach Yu Lin and introduce themselves.

Frame 3: The girls suggest playing volleyball.

Frame 4: Juliana throws the ball to Kate, but Kate doesn’t catch the ball. It hits her on the head. Kate loses her glasses.

Frame 5: Yu Lin notices Kate’s glasses under a chair.

Frame 6: Yu Lin gives the glasses to Kate and Kate thanks her.

Кадр 1: Отец Юй Лин отвозит ее в новую школу и прощается. Она напугана, потому что у нее нет друзей в школе. Ее отец пытается ее подбодрить.

Кадр 2: Две девочки подходят к Юй Лин и представляются.

Кадр 3: Девочки предлагают поиграть в волейбол.

Кадр 4: Джулиана бросает мяч Кейт, но Кейт не ловит мяч. Он попадает ей в голову. Кейт теряет очки.

Кадр 5: Юй Лин замечает очки Кейт под стулом.

Кадр 6: Юй Лин отдает очки Кейт, и Кейт благодарит ее.

ask to story

Frame 1:
What day is it? (It’s Monday.)
Where are Yu Lin and her dad? (They’re in the schoolyard.)
Why is Yu Lin scared? (She’s scared because she’s a new student and she hasn’t got any friends at the school.)

Frame 2:
What do Kate and Juliana do? (They welcome Yu Lin to the school.)
Where is Yu Lin from? (She’s from China.)
Is Yu Lin’s mother Chinese? (No, she isn’t. She is British.)
Where is Juliana from? (She’s from Brazil.)

Frame 3:
Does Yu Lin like volleyball? (Yes, she does.)
What does Juliana suggest? (She suggests playing volleyball.)

Frame 4:
What is Juliana doing? (She’s throwing the ball.)
Does Kate catch the ball? (No, she doesn’t.)
Why can’t Kate see? (Because she hasn’t got her glasses.)

Frame 5:
Who finds Kate’s glasses? (Yu Lin.)
Does Yu Lin like volleyball? (Yes, she does.) Where are they? (They’re under the chair.)

Frame 6:
What does Yu Lin do? (She gives Kate her glasses.)
What does Kate do? (She thanks Yu Lin.)​
