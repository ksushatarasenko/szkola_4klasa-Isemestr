Activity 3 - Listen and say True or False.

Listening transcript

Mrs Richards: Good morning, children. Let’s go on a school trip.
Children: Yes! Where, Mrs Richards?
Mrs Richards: Let’s visit Asheville Forest.
Anthony: How do we get there, Mrs Richards?
Mrs Richards: By train!
Children: Yeahhhhhh!!!!
Anthony: How long does it take by train?
Mrs Richards: It takes about 45 minutes, Anthony.
Anthony: What time does the train leave?
Mrs Richards: The train leaves at ten o’clock in the morning. OK, Anthony?
Anthony: We leave at ten o’clock. The trip takes about 45 minutes. So we arrive at Asheville Forest at 10 .45. And how much is the ticket?
Mrs Richards: It’s £ 3.00. Is that all, Anthony?
Anthony: £ 3.00? That’s cheap. Thank you, Mrs Richards.
Mrs Richards: You’re welcome, Anthony.

1. It takes about 50 minutes to get to Asheville Forest.

2. The train leaves at 09.20 in the morning.

3. It arrives at Asheville Forest at 10.45.

4. The ticket is £4.00.
